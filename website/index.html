What it is

Turn scattered thoughts into a clear throughline.

连点成线 / connect the points

Nous is for the moments when your thinking is spread across conversations, notes, half-decisions, and project fragments.

It keeps those pieces in one place and helps them find each other, so a new thought can build on the context you already earned.

Project mode

Project keeps the next move visible.

Use Project when you are trying to make something happen. The goal, chat, notes, and useful context stay close enough that you can move without reassembling your mind every time.

Memory

It remembers the context you would otherwise lose.

Not everything deserves to become a task, doc, or folder. Nous keeps useful fragments close: decisions, questions, notes, and themes that keep coming back.

01 / CAPTURE

Everything can start as a thought.

Chat with the assistant, write a note, or collect project context without deciding where it belongs before you begin.

02 / RECALL

Ask from where you are.

When a question needs history, Nous brings back related notes and past conversations without making you search first.

03 / REFLECT

See what keeps returning.

Repeated patterns, old decisions, and quiet contradictions become easier to notice before they disappear into the day.

Galaxy view

Galaxy reveals the shape of your thinking.

Project helps you push forward. Galaxy lets you zoom out. Related notes and conversations gather into constellations, turning forgotten fragments into visible connections.
